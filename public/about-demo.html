About Apex Bank (Demonstration Project)

Apex Bank is a fictional brand used for an educational and portfolio web application. This site simulates banking dashboards for learning and UI showcase purposes only.

  • Not affiliated with any real financial institution
  • Does not offer real accounts, loans, wire transfers, or custody of funds
  • Login forms authenticate against a demo database with pre-seeded test credentials
  • Intended for developers, students, and reviewers evaluating a UI/UX project
